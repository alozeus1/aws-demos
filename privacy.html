Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Web Forx Student Portal collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our site and services. It is intended as a general reference document and should be tailored to your organization before use in production.

Information We Collect

  • Registration details submitted through our forms (e.g., name, email, student ID, department).
  • Technical data such as browser type, device identifiers, and interaction logs for troubleshooting.
  • Aggregated metrics that help us monitor system performance and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain student records within the portal.
  • To respond to support inquiries and improve platform usability.
  • To safeguard the service, including detecting misuse or security concerns.

Your Choices

You may request access to or deletion of your record by contacting our support team. Use of the site is voluntary, and you may discontinue use at any time. If you are using this project as a template, replace this section with your organization's specific instructions and contact details.

Data Protection

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards appropriate for a learning environment. For production use, ensure encryption at rest and in transit, apply least-privilege access, and maintain backups in accordance with your compliance requirements.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or would like to exercise a privacy request, please reach out via the contact options listed on our Contact Us page.
